CHAPTER 1 · THE CANCUN SHOULDER

When Cancun package deals actually drop.

We tracked 312 Cancun all-inclusive package deals across 18 providers from January 2024 through April 2026. Two weeks of the year consistently show the lowest prices.

The first window: April 20 – May 5

Easter and spring break are over. Summer family bookings haven't ramped. Hotels that ran high occupancy through March are hungry for late April. Package prices drop 28% on average vs. the March peak.

The median 4-night all-inclusive package in late April: $512. Same package in March: $710.

The second window: September 5 – 25

Late hurricane season. Fewer travelers. Resorts discount aggressively. The caveat is weather risk — this is when the deals are real but so is the chance of a rained-out week.

THE CATCH

Hurricane insurance is not optional in September. Budget $40-80 for travel insurance with weather coverage, or book refundable rates. Both eat into the deal — math it into your number before you book.

Weeks to avoid

Thanksgiving week, December 20 – January 3, and Presidents' Day week. Package prices run 35-60% above annual median. Even our "good deals" that week are just regular prices on the other 49 weeks.
